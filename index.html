REST OF THE COUNTRIES

The world is home to 195 countries spread across seven continents, each rich in culture, history, and landscapes. Asia, the largest and most populous continent, boasts countries like China, India, and Japan. Africa, known for its diverse wildlife and deep-rooted traditions, includes nations such as Nigeria, Egypt, and South Africa. In North America, economic powerhouses like the USA and Canada stand alongside culturally vibrant Mexico. South America is famous for the Amazon rainforest, the Andes mountains, and countries like Brazil and Argentina. Europe blends historical charm with modern innovation, featuring iconic nations like the UK, France, Germany, and Italy. Australia (Oceania) consists of Australia, New Zealand, and numerous Pacific islands, while Antarctica, the frozen continent, remains largely uninhabited except for researchers.
